My name is Viktor Bakai, I am 40 years old. I would like to work as a photographer in a studio as a photo retoucher, studio photographer, assistant or something similar. I took two photography courses in a photo studio where I became richer with a lot of information and experience. Since then, I have been continuously practicing and developing myself in the field of photography, reading books and watching videos as well. I know and use Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Lightroom at a strong user level, I have learned a lot about different lighting tools such as softboxes, screens and their setup, light meter usage, light quality, properties, color temperature, etc. I became acquainted with portrait, building, macro, wildlife, architecture and object photography. I take a lot of pictures with my DSLR camera to practice and further develop myself in photography. I am fluent in English, I using it on a daily basis, mainly orally, sometimes in writing. I would really like and enjoy having the opportunity to meet new people every day in the studio and see unexpected situations as a challenge, I would also improve myself in the field of sales. My goal is to work as a photographer in the future because I really like and enjoy doing it. I am learning web development as well at the moment in HTML, CSS and Javascript languages. I am able to create websites like this one.
