Hoya Filter Kit (UV, Polarizer, ND)

  • An extremely useful filter kit for digital SLR cameras. Comprising of three round screw-in Hoya filters, an UltraViolet blocking UV(C) Multicoated filter, a Circular Polarising filter and a Neutral Density NDx8 filter.
  • The three filters are supplied in a handy folding pouch case which can accomodate up to 4 filters in total.
  • These filters can be used for everyday and creative photography and they also give protection to your valuable lens;
  • UV filters prevent dust and dirt from coming into contact with the lens. Essentially, this protective cover shields the lens;
  • A polarizing filter on your camera helps reduce shiny reflections and can darken blue skies and saturate certain colors;
  • The advantage of a neutral density filter is that it reduces the amount of light getting through to the sensor of your camera
